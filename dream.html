Cole World

Dreamville is a records label that J.Cole is signed on to. The crew is very small only including 6 artists. The artists included in this label are Bas, Cozz, Omen, Ron Gilmore, and Elite. The label was founded in 2007 and owned by J. Cole himself and Ibrahim Hamad, who is also the President of the Dreamville Records. The label includes Hip-Hop only.
Currently, the Dreamville Record Label has two albums that include all six of the artist in the label. The first album is called Revenge of the Dreamers and the second album is called Revenge of the Dreamers 2.
